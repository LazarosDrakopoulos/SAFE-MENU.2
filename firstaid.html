Food Allergy First Aid

If someone experiences a food allergy reaction, here’s what you should do:

  • Stay calm and assess the situation.
  • If mild symptoms (rash, itching), monitor and give antihistamines if prescribed.
  • For severe reactions (difficulty breathing, swelling, dizziness), call emergency services immediately.
  • Use an epinephrine auto-injector (EpiPen) if available and prescribed.
  • Keep the person lying down and elevate legs if possible.
  • Do not give food or drink if the person is having trouble swallowing.
  • Stay with them until help arrives.
